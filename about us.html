Education Redefined

Toddlers&Tweens is a reputed school with a history of long years that have turned out excellent calibre of learners with a strong foundation and positive attitude towards learning.

Today the Toddlers&Tweens has evolved into magnificent premises, a new building with state-of-the-art facilities, an open door to excellence in the field of education.

Haven boasts of an International Pre-school for the 2 to 6 yr age group, meeting the diverse needs of learners from different nationalities.

The sector focuses on the learning process, which is activity-based, where every concept is taught through hands-on experiences, observation, logic and individual attention in a rich environment.
Pre-primary Facility of ‘The Learning Framework’ for Early Childhood Development, a product of The National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC) is available for those interested in the local curriculum of Uganda.

This sector of the school is curriculum based, results-oriented and the outcome focuses on what the child will be as a result of the learning process. The program uses a learner-centered approach using Themes as a medium for knowledge in a conducive environment where the leraner develops Life Skills.

The school also offers Primary Education of Uganda for learners 6 to 12 yrs, P1 to P6. Today, education has taken on a new meaning, different approaches are merged and integrated to suit a culture and presented to the learners to become achievers.

The curriculum is project-based. Good grooming, eloquent expression, logic and reasoning, are some of the qualities the
primary school learners will reflect by the time they leave the school.

Personality of the school: When you walk into the school, there is a noticeable feeling of warmth.

The untiring efforts to make Toddlers&Tweens Haven school into an Haven with the beliefs that “stars will shine” has taken shape now.

Advancement feeds excellence in education and the educational program itself supports development efforts. The levels of development a child undergoes early in life is the level of success that child will experience later in life.
