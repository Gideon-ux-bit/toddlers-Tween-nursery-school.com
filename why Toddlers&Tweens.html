Why The Toddlers&Tweens School?

Toddlers&Tweens Haven school provides a purposeful, productive and comprehensive education of the highest order. All decisions are aligned with the mission and vision of the school that uses staff and classroom time more efficiently, purposefully and effectively keeping in line with the “nobel purpose” of education.

We value and nurture:

The happiness of children, families and staff.
Caring staff who know the children very well, who identify and develop talents and interests.
The development of self confidence, self motivation and independence.
By maximizing students individual achievement by promoting self esteem
Equality and respect individual differences
Exciting but safe and secure spaces indoor and outdoor, where children can explore, discover and experiment.
